  Capybaras are the largest rodents in the world, known for their semi-aquatic lifestyle and social nature. They are native to South America and are often found near bodies of water, where they swim and graze on vegetation. With their unique appearance and friendly demeanor, capybaras have become popular animals in zoos and have gained a dedicated fan base worldwide. Capybaras are known to form close-knit social groups, often seen lounging together in large numbers, creating an adorable spectacle for onlookers.
